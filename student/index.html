Student Life

Thriving Design and Innovation communities to stay connected & inspired

Our blossoming student life on campus is enriched with inclusivity in diversity. Students across different streams on campus are encouraged to engage through various events.

Interdisciplinary experience

There are an array of minors offered by Somaiya Vidyavihar that the student can choose along with the major. It's a huge academic opportunity for students to widen their horizons.

Maker Mela

Asia's Biggest Maker Gathering which annually hosts about 300+ global innovators. It is the premier event for grassroot innovations. Makers get an opportunity to pitch their ideas to eminent angel investors, VC's, government officials and industry. Over the past 6 years, 1500+ innovators from over 15 countries have participated.

Darwin

India's Biggest Evolutionary Movement in Biology which annually hosts about 101 Darwinians & 250+ Biohackers globally. It is an international conference for bio-enthusiasts and designed to promote the Biohacking movement in India. Over the past 2 years, 400+ Bio-enthusiasts from over 6 countries have participated.

TEDxSomaiyaVidyavihar

A platform where community-driven people showcase their stories that leads change in attitudes, lives and ultimately, the world. Over the past 3 years, 500+ attendees have participated.

Facebook Developer Circles Mumbai

It is a community of innovators, where aspiring and experienced developers receive free tools to build new skills, grow ideas and boost their careers. Over the past 3 years, 4000 developers have participated.

Enroot Mumbai

A group of passionate and motivated individuals who wish to learn & share their knowledge & apply it for development of Mumbai. Over the past 2 years, 400 developers have participated.

Fitness

Somaiya Vidyavihar is known for its state-of-the-art sports facilities. Some of the amenities include Athletic synthetic track football pitch, Badminton court, Tennis court, Squash courts, Basketball court, Volleyball courts, and a well-equipped Gymnasium. Somaiya Sports Academy actively organises events across the year and nurtures a thriving sports community.

Canteens

You do not want to miss out on our canteens. Our students are happy and healthy with the nutritious lip-smacking cuisines. The 3 canteens are constantly buzzing with life, food and conversations. They also act as a good networking space, as they accommodate people from across colleges.

Environment

Join a collaborative, happy, impact driven, and stimulating atmosphere where you feel motivated to come in everyday!
