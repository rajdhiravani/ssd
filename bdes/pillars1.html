Curriculum Framework

Our curriculum empowers our students with several Design skills, Technologies, Business nuances and real world connect that enable building solutions (product or tangible solutions , screen or graphical solutions, and strategic or service oriented solutions) in diverse thrust areas.

Thrust areas: Healthcare, Robotics, Mobility, Education, Entertainment, Fin-tech, Gaming, Animation, Governance, Sustainability, Biotech, Agri-tech, Food & Nutrition, etc.

In 4 years, students will carve their own Product Design journey depending on their interests and contemporary needs by selecting courses & thrust areas. Our curriculum ensures fine balance between exploration and deep diving.
